A few words

The POPLAVSKY GROUP architecture and design studio offers its services in development of interior and exterior design projects! We see our mission in creating a quality product that meets all the client's requirements, fits the timescale and complies with all the relevant standards. For many years our projects we used for construction and finishing works. All leading specialists of our studio are experienced people with proper education.

We have all the necessary licenses and experience in getting PS&E approval from different supervisory authorities. We not only make design projects – we also build, renovate and carry out finishing works.

Let's get acquainted! We are Vasilii and Olga – the co-founders and architects of the Poplavsky Group Studio, brother and sister. We were born in a creative family, from our childhood we were instilled with a sense of beauty, the basics of composition, the history of architecture, etc. When it was the time to choose a future profession we didn’t have any hesitations. Both of us went through a labour-intensive learning process, we are certified specialists in architecture and urban planning (VSTU), for about 10 years in the profession.

Of course, it is not only two of us, we have a time-tested, qualified team of specialists.

At the moment Poplavsky Group is carrying out projects mainly in Moscow and the Moscow region, St. Petersburg and the Leningrad region, Voronezh and the Voronezh region.

This is the main geography of our creative activity, however we have architectural and design objects in other cities. We are working and are ready to work in any city in Russia and all over the world.

Poplavsky Group's mission is to create beauty and harmony around. We are convinced that an object with any budget can be qualitatively implemented due to the trust of the client and the creative approach from our part! We are waiting for you!
