01

Professional Consultation

Price starts at 7$ per hour.

When is a consultation necessary?

1.) If you are thinking which apartment will be optimal for you at the stage of choosing a real estate. If you want to compare several options for planning solutions. Our designer will help you and suggest which space is more suitable for your needs and lifestyle.

2.) You have just bought a property. You are not going to order an interior design project, but you don’t want to make gross mistakes in the process of renovation. Our team will answer your questions, discuss possible interesting solutions and advise on how to save money, time and spare your nerves!

3.) You have purchased a land plot. You do not know where to start the construction of the future building and the design of the adjacent territory, you cannot decide on the budget, you are confused in the relevant regulations. We will help you with these questions and advice on the areas and specialists to work with, the amount of time and money to be spent on the project development and will competently compile technical requirements for your project.

4.) You are already in the process of renovation or you have an apartment with a fine finish and you want to get ideas for arranging furniture, decorating space, choosing the color scheme based on your budget. We will be happy to advise you on possible planning solutions and stylistic trends!

The final price of the consultation will be calculated individually, depending on the task our team is facing!

02

Architectural Design

The architectural project development is a challenging and time-consuming task. This is one of the most complicated and attention-requiring processes and services that we carry out. An architectural project includes many sections and subsections and requires interaction between a large team of specialists (from geodesists to engineers and designers themselves). In some cases for individual construction you need a “light version” project, that does not require a lot of approvals and time costs. We cannot say the same about a shopping center or a multi-storey residential building projects development. For this reason, in this section we do not indicate the minimum cost of the project and do not describe in detail all the stages of work.

Instead, we invite you to a free consultation, where we will gladly listen to you, discuss an approximate price of your architectural project and draw up a draft of the technical requirements for you.

03

Residential Interiors

Price starts at 20$ per hour.

Work stages:

1.) Acquaintance, first discussion to collect information.
We are all different, we have our own individual character traits and interests. For the most efficient and quick work on the project the Poplavsky Group team gets to know you better, asking about your hobbies, favourite styles, necessary storage spaces, your preferences in choosing furniture, equipment, etc.

2.) Planning and exploring the space.
We offer you several options for planning with arrangement of furniture, taking into account all your wishes. Together we choose the variant you like most and give it a final touch.

3.) 3D renderings / collages.
All of us fall in love with our eyes, that’s true. Visual image is the easiest thing for us to perceive. We will offer you photorealistic pictures of the future space or collages (for those who have rich imagination and want to save a little on the project). This stage will help to form a clear idea of the design and the chosen planning solution. At the same stage we select furniture, finishing materials and equipment according to the approved project.

4.) Construction drawings.
We develop an album of professional construction drawings for the subsequent implementation of the project, accessible and understandable to any competent builder.

The project supervision is possible in different cities and countries, its price is discussed individually. All stages of work are supported by an agreement with terms and obligations.
