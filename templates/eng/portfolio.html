Our studio develops various architectural objects: serviced apartments, houses, multi-storey apartment buildings, sports facilities, public buildings, retail areas, etc. We carry out the entire design cycle from an initial concept to construction drawings with a complete set of plans, specifications, and estimates.

We are also experienced in undergoing PS&E expertise and approval by supervisory authorities.

We also carry out architectural supervision to make sure that the project choices are followed during all the construction process.

An integral part of the design projects of our studio are private interiors - interiors of houses and apartments. Poplavsky Group provides the creation of personalized housing with a planning solution according to individual customer requests. We create the most varied interior design of residential premises and professionally implement any ideas of our clients in any space, taking into account the allocated budget. The originality of the design solution does not depend on the area.

Professional organization of working space is important for employees and clients of any commercial establishment. Our team has extensive experience in the design development of office premises, restaurants and sales areas in compliance with all technologies and ergonomic requirements. Designers work on a project in close cooperation with architects and technologists.

People actually build structures according to our projects and build them quite successfully. Many of our projects have been implemented during our work, as well as objects in the development of which the company took a significant part.
